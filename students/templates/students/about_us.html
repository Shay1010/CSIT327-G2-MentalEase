About Us

"Helping students take care of their mental health, one day at a time."

Welcome to MentalEase! Our mission is to provide students with a safe and supportive environment to track their moods, journal their thoughts, and access mental health resources. We believe that mental wellness is essential for personal growth and academic success.

Our team consists of dedicated professionals and volunteers who are passionate about student mental health. We provide curated wellness guides, emergency hotlines, and tools to help students understand and improve their emotional wellbeing.

We hope MentalEase can be your companion on your journey to self-care, mindfulness, and emotional resilience.
